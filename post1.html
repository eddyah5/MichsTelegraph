MASSIVE FIRE AT SILICON VALLEY

Massive and dangerous inferno at silicon valley wrecks havoc and destroys lifes

Fire rages on unrelectlessly as fire fighters struggle to put it off at 103 Ave., East London Street, Silicon Valley, California. It is reported that 20 are confirmed dead, 150 injured.

The fire started as a result of gas leak due to lack of proper maintenance and negligence on the side of the properpty managers. DotProp Ltd, the property management company denies any fault or wrong doing on their part, claiming the incident is as a result of unforseen circumstances.
